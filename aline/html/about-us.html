Hiring conditions

Full-time, part-time, hourly workload at a reasonable rate. Our team of talented developers always uses best practices and writes clean code. We provide high-quality work, honest feedback and always follow the agreed timeframe.

When customers work with remote developers, they often worry about product delivery. Thanks to face-to-face communication channels such as Skype or any other preferred messenger our customers are informed about our workflow and development process.

Companies are often concerned about the difficulty of controlling remote workers’ performance. To make this process transparent, we are using different tracking tools, e.g. Toggl, DeskTime, TimeDoctor, Teamwork, etc. Thanks to these tools we can provide feedback that shows the amount of time spent on every task.

If your team doesn’t understand your needs properly, the project is not going to turn out great. Effective communication is the key to resolving these kind of issues. Our company has a significant amount of experience of implementing projects for customers from all over the world providing high quality outsourcing services.

Find a coder establishes a virtual meeting place for companies and developers

After signing up, we'll provide employers looking to hire developers with a list of developers matching their requirements. Companies will only have restricted access to the profile of developers. We choose to only display vital information about the developer such as skills and experience level.

After confirming they would be interested to hire this particular person, they are able to press a like button. The developer will receive a message that a company is interested to hire them. Only if they respond to this message and also like the company's profile, will it be possible for both parties to connect.

At this stage the company will have full access to a developer's profile. Both companies and developers will be rated based on their response level.

This method of matchmaking used by find a coder allows for an average success rate of 86%
